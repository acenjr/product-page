Mzee Konana and Anupi's Honey is a delectable and ethically crafted honey, proudly produced by the indigenous members of the Ogiek community in Kenya. In a unique collaboration with the parent organization, Planet Plus, founded by the visionary Sarah Anupi, this honey represents a harmonious blend of tradition, sustainability, and environmental conservation.

The Ogiek people are forest-adjacent dwellers who have been intricately connected to the lush landscapes of the Mau Forest Complex for generations. Mzee Konana and Anupi's Honey emerges as a testament to their age-old beekeeping practices, carried out in harmony with nature.

This golden elixir is not just a delight for your taste buds but also plays a pivotal role in the preservation of the Mau Forest Complex. By supporting this honey, you contribute to the ongoing efforts to protect the rich biodiversity and ecosystem of one of Kenya's vital natural treasures.

Experience the sweetness of Mzee Konana and Anupi's Honey, where tradition, community, and environmental conservation converge in a jar of pure goodness.
